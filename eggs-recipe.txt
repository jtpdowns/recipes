Ingredients:
- 3-5 pastured eggs
- 3-4 ounces of extra virgin olive oil

Directions:
1) Heat stainless steel pan on low to medium heat.
2) Place down olive oil, and move it around the pan.
3) Crack and open eggs onto the pan one at a time.
4) Add salt and pepper to eggs while the whites are still liquid.
5) Wait until egg whites turn opaque, then flip.
6) Wait until egg sizzles or bubbles, then move onto plate.
7) Enjoy.
